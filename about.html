Our Story

How We Started

Founded in 2010, SolarPro began with a simple mission: to make clean, renewable energy accessible to everyone. Our founders, a team of engineers and environmental enthusiasts, recognized the growing need for sustainable energy solutions in a world increasingly affected by climate change.

What started as a small operation with just three employees has now grown into a leading solar installation company with over 50 dedicated professionals serving residential, agricultural, and commercial clients across the region.

Our Growth Journey

Over the years, we've installed thousands of solar power systems, helping our clients reduce their carbon footprint while saving on energy costs. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of the most trusted names in the solar industry.

Today, SolarPro continues to lead the way in solar technology, constantly researching and implementing the latest advancements to provide our clients with the most efficient and reliable solar solutions available.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy by providing high-quality, affordable solar solutions that reduce carbon emissions and energy costs for our clients.

Our Vision

To create a world where clean, renewable energy is the standard, not the alternative. We envision communities powered by the sun, with energy independence and environmental sustainability at their core.
